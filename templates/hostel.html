Hostel Haven
The hostel facilities at Sri Vasavi Engineering College are designed to provide a comfortable and conducive environment for students.The hostel experience is aimed at fostering a sense of community and helping students maintain a balanced lifestyle while pursuing their academic goals.

Gents Hostel

The gents hostel in SVEC provides spacious rooms with basic amenities, ensuring a comfortable stay for students. It features 24/7 security, well-maintained common areas, and access to study rooms. The hostel fosters a disciplined yet friendly environment, perfect for academic focus and social interactions.

Ladies Hostel

The ladies hostel in SVEC offers a safe and peaceful atmosphere for female students, with well-ventilated rooms and essential facilities. Hostel Rules & Regulations

  • 1. Check-In Time: 2:00 PM onwards.
  • 2. Check-Out Time: 7:30 PM.
  • 3. Cleanliness: Students must keep their area clean and dispose of trash in designated bins.
  • 4. Security: Always lock your door and keep valuables in the provided lockers.
  • 5. Noise: Avoid loud noise or music that can disturb other residents.
  • 6. Ragging: Ragging in any form in the hostel is illegal. Sent to jail range from 6 months to 10 years in jail, with fines up to Rs. 50,000/-.
