Technical Director Profile

Sri Checka Apparao, is the son of our society member Sri Ch VR Ramana Murthy. He completed his B.Tech, with Computer Science and Systems Engineering from GITAM, Vishakapatnam. Did his Master's Degree in Computer Science the USA. He has worked in IT industry for 8 years in various positions like(Project Manager, Architect, System Administrator, HR Manager,etc.) in US. He then became entrepreneur and owned several retail franchised businesses for 9 years in United States. Having lot of experience in technological field and administration, he took charge as a Director(Technical) of Sri Vasavi Engineering College in 2016. Since the last three years he is involved in the day to day activities and he is a part of the policy changes and stream lining the process for the growth of the college.
